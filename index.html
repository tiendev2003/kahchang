Founder's message:

Starting from students studying in an international environment, we have had the opportunity to meet, interact and exchange with many friends from countries around the world. This helps us understand more about the world's preferences and styles.

In the early days of February 2024, after consulting with many international friends, we realized that they chose Vietnam as a study abroad destination partly because of its wild beauty and majestic nature. This is completely understandable because Vietnam's location and climate are very suitable for experiencing, exploring and relaxing activities. "I feel surprised and excited when admiring the beauty of the mountains on the seas of Vietnam. They help me see how wonderful nature really is" - is what a friend from Russia told us. I. Statistics from Google's destination search trend tracking tool - Google Destination Insights show that the number of international searches for Vietnam tourism is growing by about 50 - 75%, the 4th highest increase in the world.

At first it was an adventure, a passion to explore the beauty of our homeland, but over time, we felt we needed to take more specific actions. Help many friends know and choose Vietnam as an option to explore among the countless wonders of the world. We joined hands to build an advertising website about homeland tourism.
